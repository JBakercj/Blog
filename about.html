About
I'm a life-long Utahn and a first-time home owner in Lehi as of 2021. I live with my wife, two kids (2 and 5), cousin-in-law (17), and my two dogs (Golden Retriever and Siberian Husky).
Education
I started college at USU to study computer science. Near the end of my degree, I transferred from USU to attend UVU and graduated from UVU with a B.S. in criminal justice with an emphasis in law enforcement and a minor in computer science. After my Bachelor's degree, I went back to UVU and graduated with an M.S. in Cybersecurity.
Experience
I am currently the At-Large member of the Utah Libertarian Party's Executive Committee and have served in that elected role since April, 2021. I worked in software QA with smart IoT devices for 7 years. I also worked as a cybersecurity engineer with smart IoT devices for 3 years and worked with setting IoT product standards and policies. As of recently, I am a cybersecurity lead/architect handling all product security for an advanced robotics company. My B.S. in criminal justice and my work in corporate cybersecurity policy gives me a much needed and unique perspective that I could bring to the state legislature.
